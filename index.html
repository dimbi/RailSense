More bumpy routes means unpleasant rides? Em, not really.

Our reseach hinted that bumpiness of a route shows positive correlation, but not enough significant (statistically) to prove the hypothesis. Fluctuation in level of attention were mainly related to specific events during rides.

Reporting application.

Railsense platforms allow you to not only report the automated brainwave and accelerometer but also manual input made by user according to the encountered disturbances.

Subway Distraction.

Some complaints are not related to the bumpiness or accelerometer e.g. Street Entertainer, sound of the tunnel scratch.It is not the high level of attention that are clustered around the point of events, but the high frequency of state changes. This mean that a lot of dots in the attention maps are more likely to respresent possible complaints made by the passangers, rather than fixed/stagnant high attention plot.
